Dialpad for HubSpot CRM by Sandeza

Overview

Dialpad for HubSpot CRM is a CTI integration that allows users to make calls directly from HubSpot, synchronize contacts, and manage customer interactions without leaving the CRM.

Features

  • Click-to-Call
  • HubSpot Contact Sync
  • Dialpad Softphone
  • OAuth Authentication
  • Call Activity Tracking
  • CRM Dashboard
